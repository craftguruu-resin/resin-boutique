Homepage hero

When custom hero is on, the entire homepage hero (headline block + product visual) is replaced by your images—great for offers. Use file uploads or full https URLs (e.g. Cloudinary). Turn off custom hero to restore the built-in layout.

Guest site: custom hero

When Custom hero on homepage is off, guests see the original split hero (copy on the left, showcase on the right). Your slides and URLs stay saved here so you can publish them again anytime. On the live site, each slide is scaled to fit the full hero block—your whole image stays visible (letterboxing may appear on very wide or very tall uploads instead of cropping).

Homepage playback

Rotate all slides runs your full-width hero images in order (default 5 seconds). Transitions move the current image left and bring the next from the right. Set fixed hero on a row shows only that image until you return to carousel.

Upload many slides (5–20 images)

One batch adds at least five images (up to twenty) with the same motion preset. They appear in order after your existing slides.

Add slides from image URLs (Cloudinary, CDN, etc.)

Paste one URL per line (or separate with commas). Each must start with https:// or http://. Up to 25 at once. Same motion preset applies to all new rows in this save.
