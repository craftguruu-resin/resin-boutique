Categories & navigation

Choose Resin products (storefront catalogue), Resin raw material (raw shop sidebar), or Resin photo frame (photo frames page links). Create with names and image URLs; edits and deletes sync to the guest site after the next page load or catalog refresh.

Create category or line

Pick a domain, enter names, then paste image URLs (https or a path like /media/…). Subcategory is optional for resin products and raw material bases.

Edit or delete

Select domain, category, and optional line. Names update from your selection; use Load selection to refresh image and shop link from the server, then save. Subcategories for storefront categories also include any lines used on products, even if they were not listed in the original catalogue.
