Who we are today

Craftguru operates from Jaipur with a bench-first mindset: colour chemistry, controlled exotherm, and finish QA matter as much as the final silhouette. We serve retail buyers who want one statement piece and wholesale partners who need predictable spec, clean packaging, and honest timelines—especially through heat, monsoon transit, and busy retail seasons.

Our online catalog mirrors what we pour in the studio—when you pick a size on a product page, you are choosing the same pour rules we use for stockists. Account sign-in (phone + OTP) lets you save progress after checkout on this device; Instagram and WhatsApp stay the fastest lanes for sizing doubts and custom briefs.

What we pour

Lines we ship today include (and keep growing with the bench):

  • Resin coasters, trays, and cutlery or tissue holders for tables that see daily use.
  • Wall clocks and mantra frames where depth and rim-light read clean from a distance.
  • Pooja plates, ring platters, varmala preservation, and customised frames for rituals and milestones.
  • Keychains, nameplates, and gallery pieces sized for gifting or retail shelves.

Our process

Crystal-clear epoxy, UV-aware pigments, staged metallics, and slow cures keep surfaces glassy instead of foggy. Large faces get thermal management; thin edges get craze-aware handling. Every SKU path ends with a light check and fingertip pass—same bar for one-offs and repeat wholesale batches.

Sustainability & packaging

Scrap from larger pours is recast into smaller SKUs where chemistry allows. Packing uses recycled paper where we can, corner armour only where resin needs it, and minimal single-use plastic—enough to survive Indian courier networks, not more.
